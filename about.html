ABOUT US

FULLERS JEWELRY: serving DFW since 1949

our family serving yours for over 70 years

fullers jewelry

Fuller’s Jewelry has been adorning Dallas residents with nostalgic jewelry since the late ‘40s. It’s been an honor and a privilege to serve residents just like you for nearly five generations.

Our family business was built on integrity, respect, and our love for our clients. Even though these were the building blocks that our business was founded on so many years ago– these principles are still the heartbeat behind our work today.

Back in 1949, our founding family– the Fullers– knew that Dallas-Fort Worth needed a local jewelry store that people could trust. They needed a warm place for folks to visit, browse, and purchase timeless pieces.

Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

Our customers are the most precious part of our business. Even more valuable than gemstones and gold– are our cherished clients.

Your satisfaction is our number one priority.

Because we focus on building high-quality relationships with our customers, we’ve been sustained with lasting business. Our generous clients trust us and return to us with any of their jewelry needs. It’s because of this that we’ve been able to build such a solid customer base.

The Dallas/Fort Worth community is our home, and our customers are our friends and neighbors. And like a friendly neighbor, we want you to know that you can always trust Fuller’s Jewelry for the best value in jewelry anywhere.

Unlike most jewelers, we actually listen to you and implement your feedback. That’s the main reason we’re able to offer such a superior selection of jewelry. Not to mention, our pieces are all backed by the Fuller Family Commitment to your total satisfaction.

When you do business with the Fuller’s Jewelry team, you can expect to be treated with respect and kindness. We’ve been in business long enough to know the intricacies of all types of jewelry.

From finding an engagement ring, to a birthday necklace, to even repairing jewelry– Fuller’s promises to take delicate care of you and your timeless treasures.

We’re more than just your friendly neighborhood jeweler. At Fuller’s Jewelry, you’ll be treated like family.
